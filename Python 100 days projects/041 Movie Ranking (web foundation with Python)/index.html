The Best Movies According to TonyVSK

My top 3 movies of all-time

Inglourious Basterds

A Tarantino film that takes place during the Second World War. In the film, American soldiers infiltrate German troops, disguising themselves as soldiers of the German Reich, to attack them from within.

The Godfather

A cinema classic. A film about Don Corleone, an Italian who came from Sicily and lived in the United States during his childhood, and ended up forming one of the largest mafia empires in America.

Forrest Gump

A movie about 30 years (from the 1950s through the early 1980s) of the life of an intellectually disabled man (played by Tom Hanks) in an unlikely fable.
